About

I'm an LDT student who likes taking a messy topic and figuring out the clearest way to teach it. Whether that's turning a personal story into a short film, building a self-paced lesson that walks a freshman through their first credit card, or writing alt text so a screen reader user doesn't miss the point of a chart.

Most of what's here started as coursework, but I built each piece the way I'd build it for a real client: with a real audience in mind, a clear learning objective, and a tool chosen on purpose rather than by default. I'm looking for an internship where I can keep doing that at a bigger scale.
